# Excel-Project---Impact-of-AI-on-Digital-Media
Interactive Excel dashboard analyzing the impact of AI across industries through KPIs, various charts, and slicers.


This project presents an Excel-based interactive dashboard that analyzes the impact of Artificial Intelligence (AI) across multiple industries. It highlights trends in AI adoption, revenue growth, customer trust, job loss, and regulation status using visual charts and dynamic filters.

The dashboard is designed to help users understand how AI is shaping industries, boosting productivity, altering workforce demands, and influencing customer trust in various AI tools.


**Project Objective**

To explore and visualize the multi-dimensional impact of AI in industries by analyzing:
- AI adoption rates
- Revenue growth due to AI
- Human–AI collaboration
- Customer trust in popular AI tools


**Tools & Techniques Used** - Microsoft Excel, Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, Dashboard layout & design


**Data Overview**

- Number of Records: 200
- Total Columns: 12
    - Country
    - Year
    - Industry
    - AI Adoption Rate (%)
    - AI-Generated Content Volume (TBs per year)
    - Job Loss Due to AI (%)
    - Revenue Increase Due to AI (%)
    - Human–AI Collaboration Rate (%)
    - Top AI Tools Used
    - Regulation Status
    - Consumer Trust in AI (%)
    - Market Share of AI Companies (%)


**Insights & Observations:**

- AI adoption continues to rise across key sectors, especially gaming and education.
- Revenue impact is highest in automotive and media industries.
- Customer trust in AI is distributed among generative AI, chatbots, and image tools.
- Job loss is more noticeable in manufacturing, finance, and media sectors.
- Regulations vary across industries, reflecting different levels of risk and dependency.


**Conclusion:**

This dashboard demonstrates how AI is increasingly influencing industries—improving revenue, enabling collaboration, and reshaping workforce dynamics. The Excel dashboard provides a clear, interactive, and data-driven view of AI’s evolving role.
